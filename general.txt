naming conventions

variables - camelCase - myVariable
constants - UPPERCASE_UNDERSCORE - MY_CONSTANT
functions - camelCase, verbs - getMyFunction
classes - UpperCamelCase - MyClass (class instances are camelCase)
modules/classes - lowercase_underscore-hyphen - package-name/package_name
enumeration types - UpperCase_Underscore - DayOfWeek/Day_Of_Week
file names - lowercase_underscore-hyphen - my_file/my-file
